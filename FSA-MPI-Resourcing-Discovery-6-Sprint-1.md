## Next week (11-15 Nov)

Understanding the user experience and service context will be our main focus over this sprint week. The diary is full of interviews lined up with Team Leaders and Area Managers, some of which will spill over into next week. We'll start building up a rich view of who the users are, how they work with resourcing tools and what their needs are. 

The insights we gather will also help us start understanding the service flow and constraints. As common pain points emerge, we'll begin identifying areas we may need to drill into or test further in coming weeks. 

We'll continue to do desk research and build up the stakeholder map, ahead of lining up further interviews, particularly with stakeholders outside of Field Ops and beyond the FSA.

In anticipation of that we're also sketching out some of the code and data interfaces that are likely needed to support integration of SOR, rotas and timesheets, so that we can hit the ground running for the second phase of discovery.
