# FSA weeknotes

Every week, our Delivery Managers write up a short _weeknote_ to say what their team has been up that week and what's planned for the next. It's our way of working in the open to keep everyone informed about progress and what we're learning as we go.

NB: We have a few [rules](Weeknote-rules) to ensure we remain mindful of what we're posting in the open.

The Incidents Management Service **detects, prevents, receives, investigates and manages** incidents following an actual or potential threat to the safety, quality or integrity of food and/or animal feed.
